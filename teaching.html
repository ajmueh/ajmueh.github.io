Teaching

Below are some of the courses, with brief discriptions, I've taught as a teaching fellow at Yale.

Introduction to R

We teach students how to think analytically/statistically and process and analyze their data in the programming language R. Taught for three semesters.

Ecology & Conservation of Tropical Forests

This course covered the fundamental ecology of tropical forests, mechanisms generating biodiversity, and the challenges regarding their conservation. Taught for one semester.

Tropical Field Ecology

A joint course with F&ES masters students and E&EB undergraduates, where we studied abroad in Ecuador for two weeks, travelling to multiple ecosystems from the Andes to the Amazon and learning their ecology first-hand. Taught for one semester.

Trees: Environmental Biology & Global Significance

We are teaching students about the physiology and function of trees/forests, and the role forests play in the biosphere. Taught for one semester.
