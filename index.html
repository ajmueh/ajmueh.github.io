Andrew Muehleisen

Ecology. Data Science Education.

I'm Andrew - I received my PhD in 2020 from Yale's School of the Environment studying the ways in which environmental stresses shape forest understories (and therefore their futures). These days I'm a postdoc at the University of Oregon, where I work with 1) Lauren Hallett studying the patterns and processes underlying diversity in semi-arid grasslands, and 2) the Data Science Initiative developing and iterating on new degree programs in Data Science. I'm passionate about scientific literacy, data proficiency, causal inference, and best practices for teaching the above. I apply both theory and statistical computation in my research and my teaching.
